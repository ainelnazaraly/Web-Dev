Most visited shopping centers in Almaty

Dostyk Plaza

Thanks to architectural solutions from the famous English architectural bureau Benoy and wide offers in the sphere of shopping and leisure, DOSTYK PLAZA has become one of the favorite places of Almaty citizens and a must-visit for guests of the southern capital.

Esentai Mall

Esentai Mall is the only luxury mall in Central Asia that offers a special atmosphere for shopping and entertainment. Esentai Mall exclusively features such brands as Louis Vuitton, Prada, Dior, Moncler, as well as a three-level Saks Fifth Avenue store and a jewelry gallery.
