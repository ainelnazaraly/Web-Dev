Khachapuri

  • Time: 2h
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
salt 1 tsp
dry active yeast 1 tsp
Granulated sugar 1 tbsp
All purpose Flour 3 1/2 cups
milk 2 1/2 cups

Step 1: In the bowl of a stand mixer combine the salt, yeast, sugar, and flour.

Step 2: Heat water and milk to about 115 degrees Fahrenheit. Then pour it into the bowl of dry ingredients.

Step 3: Begin kneading the dough with the hook attachment until it's close to being smooth and elastic.

Step 4: Drizzle a little bit of olive oil onto the bottom and sides of a deep bowl. Place the dough inside the bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Set the bowl in a warm place until the dough doubles in size, about 1 hour.

Coffee Buns

  • Time: 3h
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Whole FLour 3 cups
Milk powder 16 g
Instant yeast 6g

Step 1: Combine the flour, sugar, salt, milk powder, and instant yeast in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook, or in a large bowl

Step 2: Add the lukewarm milk and mix until a rough dough ball forms

Step 3: Add the unsalted butter and knead for 10-15 minutes with the stand mixer, or approx 20 minutes by hand until smooth and elastic

Step 4: Roll into a ball, place in a bowl and cover with cling wrap
